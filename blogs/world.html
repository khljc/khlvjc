Life on this world

In the grand scheme of things, none of it matters at all, does it? What's the meaning of existence on this insignificant planet pertaining to the infinite universe? Emptiness fills most of the universe as well as our hearts, day and night, we wrestle with it. A longing for something more is present within us, nevertheless, this feeling never leaves us in spite of our efforts. This world is abundant in lustful pleasures such as drugs, alcohol, pornography, materialism, gambling, and so much more, yet, the yearning of the heart seems to stretch far beyond the edge of the universe.

Escaping the world

How do I escape? Vanity is all that exists in this world; so for what reason shall one stay?

The way out is right next to you. However, take heed, for whoever follows this path is to forgo all they have in order of entering through the narrow gate.
